ALL NEW MERCEDEZ BENZ MAYBACH

Where luxury meets automotive excellence

INTERIOR

Step inside the 2022 Maybach, and you'll be greeted by a sumptuous interior that sets new standards for luxury. The cabin is adorned with premium materials such as Nappa leather, exquisite wood veneers, and high-quality metal trims. The rear seats offer a first-class experience, with spacious legroom, heated and ventilated massaging seats, and even a refrigerator for your beverages.

FEATURES

Exquisite Craftsmanship: Maybach vehicles are known for their exceptional attention to detail and craftsmanship. Every aspect of the car's design, from the exterior to the interior, showcases meticulous workmanship. Spacious Cabin: Maybach vehicles, especially those with extended wheelbases, provide ample space for both the driver and passengers. Rear-seat passengers, in particular, enjoy a sense of opulence and roominess

PERFORMANCE

ENGINE

Advanced Technology: Maybach incorporates cutting-edge technology into its vehicles. This includes high-resolution infotainment systems, advanced driver-assistance features, and a premium sound system for an immersive driving experience

Maybach cars are equipped with powerful engines that deliver a smooth and commanding performance. The vehicles often provide a balance of power and refinement, offering a comfortable ride with ample acceleration
